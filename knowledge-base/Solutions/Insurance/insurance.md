## Frequently Asked Questions
Common questions about the insurance voice solution
How can OmniDimension help my insurance business?
You can create a voice assistant that answers policyholder questions, processes claims, collects documents, and routes calls to agents - reducing manual workload while improving response time.
Can my assistant handle multiple policy types?
Yes. You can configure it to manage auto, health, home, life, or any other policy types. Each assistant can be customized with unique scripts and workflows.
Can it process insurance claims?
Yes. The assistant can collect claim details, verify policyholder information, and escalate to adjusters or agents when required.
Does it integrate with our CRM or policy management system?
Yes. OmniDimension integrates with tools like Salesforce, HubSpot, and Zoho, syncing every interaction automatically.
How quickly can we set it up?
Setup takes about 20 seconds. You define your policy types, claims process, and CRM connections.
Can it remind customers about renewals or payments?
Yes. You can enable automated renewal reminders, premium payment follow-ups, and personalized retention messages.
